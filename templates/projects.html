Emerge Connect

I participated in an ideathon, in October 2023, where my team and I presented a concept for an online campus clinic appointment system. The main objective of the system was to simplify and streamline the booking process for students seeking medical assistance. We were only required to produce a prototype and not develop the full system; the focus was on presenting a clear and impactful idea. By improving accessibility and reducing waiting times, our idea aimed to address a common challenge faced by students. Our concept was well-received and ranked in the Top 10 among all presentations delivered on the day.

Campus Clinic Telephonic Booking System

As part of a group project for the Systems Analysis and Design module, we developed a telephonic clinic appointment booking system. The system allowed students to schedule appointments based on available nurses and time slots. It featured a database to store appointment details securely and included a notification feature that automatically alerted users once a booking was confirmed. This project emphasized practical application of system design and database integration.

Off-Grid

My group and I developed a game called Off-Grid using Scratch. Off-Grid is designed to educate users about the consequences of load shedding and to introduce them to alternative power sources that can help minimize power outages. The game targets individuals in small communities, aiming to raise awareness among those who may be unaware of alternative energy options or have received inaccurate information. By engaging players, Off-Grid seeks to foster understanding and encourage informed decisions about power usage.

According to Head (2021), load shedding began in 2007, and since then, new scheduling systems have been introduced. Our game incorporates this historical context to help users better understand the origins and ongoing challenges of load shedding. Additionally, Off-Grid highlights the benefits of various renewable energy sources, with the goal of empowering community members with practical knowledge and sustainable solutions.
